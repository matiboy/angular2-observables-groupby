Using Observables's "groupBy"

A service fakes a socket that sends data at a random interval of 1-3 seconds and pretends to have received a user's score to an imaginary game

The data is passed through groupBy to achieve the "standings" table on the LHS.

This also makes use of scan, distinctUntilChanged (with and without compare method), Subjects and BehaviorSubjects
